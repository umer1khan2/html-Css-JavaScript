Welcome to the Driving Licence Sindh

The Driving License department is an important organ of Sindh Police entrusted to issue driving license and permits to qualified drivers after ensuring sense of driving laws, driving ethics, road sense.

Driving license is an important document even more important than your national identity card as this give you authority to drive your vehicle on roads after passing through different tests which are made to give you awareness about the road ethics, road safety, the driving license is the official document which authorizes its holder to operate various types of motor vehicle on roads to which the public have access.

What We Do

Online License Verification

We have introduced the facility of online verification of driving licenses in all categories through Public app available free on Google Play Store as well as this portal. The services provided are License Verification / License Fee Structure / License History / Applicant Enquiry / Driving License Branches address / Theoretical - Road Test Preparation etc. Anyone can visit / use frequently and give feedback through this App

Learner Driving License Campaign

To facilitate general public we introduce mobile units for issuance of learners driving license on request. We conduct such activities on regular basis in Educational Institutions, Firms, Organizations and Associations on request
